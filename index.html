Company History

This company was set up due to an increasing demand, from industrial boiler companies for specific training. This has came about because there is a lack of trained engineers in the workplace with the required skills sets. We have carried out extensive research on the skills sets required by these companies. We have custom designed our courses to target these skill sets and provide the companies with the training they require.
